Financial Rewards

Pur'N'Kleen works to make you as much UEC as possible. You will make more money space trucking with us than going alone because we share resources and split expenses. Safety and Performance Bonuses are planned.

Not a miner or space trucker? No problem. We have opportunities for people that want to crew ships in their off-times, focus on trading and market exploitation, exploration of new mining spots, and defense. The main point is that since we're structured as an organization, we split our profits and take care of each other. This allows our members to explore different careers without having to worry too much about making ends meet or feel pressured to fulfill a certain role.

Why Pur'N'Kleen Company?

  • Training and Development
  • Regular Fleet Events
  • Work/Life Balance (no mandatory activities)
  • Safety and Performance Bonuses
  • Diverse and Inclusive Community
  • Free meals and Greycat rides
